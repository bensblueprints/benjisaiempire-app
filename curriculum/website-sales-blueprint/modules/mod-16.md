## Lesson 16.1 — Framework

1. **Acknowledge** — "Totally fair."
2. **Clarify** — "When you say X, do you mean…?"
3. **Reframe** — tie back to free risk reversal or revenue

## Lesson 16.2 — Top objections

- Not interested → "Worth 30 seconds — free redesign, zero invoice today."
- Send info → "Preview takes me an afternoon — need 2 min to see if you qualify."
- Have a guy → "Keep him — compare my free mockup side by side."
- No budget → "That's why today is free."
- Call back later → book exact time while on phone
