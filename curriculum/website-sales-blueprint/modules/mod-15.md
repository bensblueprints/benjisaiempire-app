## Lesson 15.1 — When to switch

| Variation | Use when |
|-----------|----------|
| Polite Neighbor | Older owners, small towns |
| Direct Closer | Busy owners, B2B |
| Curious Outsider | Skeptics, lawyers, contractors |
| Industry Specialist | After 5+ builds in one niche |
| Master (default) | When unsure |

## Lesson 15.2 — Study the PDF

**Download:** Cold Calling Script Pack (portal). Read variations 1–5 once — do not learn all five before Day 1.

## Lesson 15.3 — One variation per week

During the 30-day blueprint, pick **one** alternate variation per week max. Master Script stays the default.
