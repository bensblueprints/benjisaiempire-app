## Lesson 7.1 — Economics of free

Free removes risk. Your margin is:
- **$500** build (one-time)
- **$20/mo** hosting (recurring)
- Optional ads later

You are not charity — you are buying the conversation.

## Lesson 7.2 — Scope boundaries

**Included:** One marketing homepage redesign, mobile-friendly, deployed to preview URL, one demo call.

**Not included (unless upsold):** Unlimited revisions, e-commerce, 20 pages, copywriting from scratch, logo brand projects.

**Checklist**
- [ ] Scope written in your invoice template
- [ ] Build time cap (e.g. 48–72h from URL to preview)

## Lesson 7.3 — Ideal prospect profile

Call businesses that:
- Have a website that looks dated or broken on mobile
- Care about local customers
- Answer the phone (or have a direct owner line)

**Checklist**
- [ ] Pick one niche for Days 1–10 of the blueprint (stay focused)
