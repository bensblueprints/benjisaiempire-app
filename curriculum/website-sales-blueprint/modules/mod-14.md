## Lesson 14.1 — Read the Master Script

Open portal **Downloads** → copy **Master Script — Video Cold Call** or the script pack PDF.

Structure:
1. Crazy question opener + pause
2. Website costing them business (industry-specific word)
3. Winding down vs growing question — **shut up**
4. Two things: ads + website
5. Free build → $500 if they love it
6. Discovery: average ticket / cost to acquire a customer

## Lesson 14.2 — Practice out loud

- [ ] 10 reps without reading
- [ ] Record one rep — under 90 seconds to the free offer
- [ ] Fix where you rush pauses

## Lesson 14.3 — Pair with GHL

Every conversation: log outcome before you dial the next number. Speed beats perfection.
