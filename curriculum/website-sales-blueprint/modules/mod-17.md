## Lesson 17.1 — Gatekeeper

> "Hey — is the owner in? It's [Name] about the website — should only take 30 seconds."

If blocked: ask best time + direct email.

## Lesson 17.2 — Voicemail (under 25 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], [You] — I was on your site and had one quick idea that might bring more [customers/bookings]. I'll shoot a text — call me back at [number]. No pitch on voicemail."

## Lesson 17.3 — Same-day callback rule

If they call back within 2 hours, you answer. Momentum closes free-site yeses.
