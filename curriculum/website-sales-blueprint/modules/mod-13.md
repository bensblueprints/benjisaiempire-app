## Lesson 13.1 — List size math

30 days × 100 dials = **3,000 dials**. You need **3,000+ rows** with phone numbers before you start Day 1.

## Lesson 13.2 — Where to source

- Google Maps export / manual copy
- Industry directories
- Drive-by corridors (photos of bad sites)

Columns: Business | Phone | Website URL | Niche | Notes

## Lesson 13.3 — List hygiene

- [ ] Remove obvious wrong numbers
- [ ] Tag "has website" vs "no website" (different opener)
- [ ] Sort hottest first (ads running, bad mobile site, owner name found)
