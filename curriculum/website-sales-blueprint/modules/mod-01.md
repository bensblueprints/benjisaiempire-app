## Lesson 1.1 — Operator vs agency domain

**Pick one lane for the next 90 days:**

1. **Operator brand** — `YourName.com` (you are the product; good for YouTube + trust)
2. **Agency brand** — `SomethingDigital.com` / `SomethingSites.com` (easier to sell and later hire)

**Checklist**
- [ ] Domain idea passes the phone test (say it twice on a mock call — no spelling alphabet)
- [ ] `.com` preferred; avoid hyphens and cute TLDs for cold calls
- [ ] Name matches what you will put on invoices and GHL

## Lesson 1.2 — The offer in one sentence

Memorize this structure:

> We build your website for **free**. If you love the redesign, it's **$500 one-time** plus **$20/month** hosting. If you don't, you walk away — no hard feelings.

**Checklist**
- [ ] You can say the offer in under 15 seconds without reading
- [ ] You know what "free" includes (one redesign, one demo, defined timeline)
- [ ] You know what happens after they say yes (GHL stage: **Agreed to free site**)

## Lesson 1.3 — What you are really selling

You are not selling "a website." You are selling:
- More customers through the door
- A site that does not embarrass them
- A low-risk way to try you ($0 today)

**Homework**
- [ ] Write 3 local business types you will call first (e.g. auto, dental, restaurant)
