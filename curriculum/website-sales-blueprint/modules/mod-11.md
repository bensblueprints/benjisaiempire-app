## Lesson 11.1 — Invoice sent

Include:
- One-time website build: **$500**
- Hosting: **$20/month** (what they get: SSL, uptime, minor text edits — define it)
- Payment link + due date (7 days)

GHL stage → **Invoice sent**

## Lesson 11.2 — Follow-up cadence

- Day 0: invoice sent on call
- Day 2: text/email "any questions on the invoice?"
- Day 5: phone follow-up
- Day 7: "invoice expires Friday" line

## Lesson 11.3 — Invoice paid

When paid:
- [ ] GHL → **Invoice paid**
- [ ] Thank-you + timeline for DNS go-live
- [ ] Start Module 12 delivery checklist
