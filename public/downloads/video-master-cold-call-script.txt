The Master Script
This is the baseline. Every variation is a tuning of this. Learn this one cold before mixing in the
variations.
[Confident, warm tone. Smile before you dial.]
Hey, crazy question. [Pause. Wait up to 4 seconds. If they say nothing, keep going.]
I was, uh, looking at your website. [Pause. Short, natural.] And, uh... [Slightly hesitant, like
you are being polite about saying something awkward.] I think it's costing you some
business.
[If automotive shop:] I think it's costing you some repairs to your competitors.
[If restaurant:] I think it's costing you some reservations.
[If dentist, med spa, or salon:] I think it's costing you some bookings.
[Adapt the word to what they actually do. Put their money in the competitor pocket.]
I was just wondering, are you winding down the business, or are you trying to get more
customers through the door?
[Shut up. Let them answer. This is the most important pause of the whole call.]
Because there's two things I can do for you. One, I can run your ads and send a ton of
customers into your business. Two, I gotta get that website improved first.
But here's the thing. I'm not asking you for any money today. I'll build your website for free. If
you like the redesign, it's 500 bucks. If you don't, you can tell me to kick rocks. No hard feelings.
[Pause. Let it land. Free is the magic word.]
If you do like it, we move forward with the 500, and then we can have a quick conversation
about ads.
What's your average ticket price? [Or:] How much would you be willing to spend to land a new
customer?
[Now you are in discovery. You passed the gate. Take notes.]
